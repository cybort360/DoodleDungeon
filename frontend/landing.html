the ritual

Three steps
to glory.

No gym membership. No app store. Just paper, your phone, and the will to not be embarrassed by a goblin.

01

Doodle your dungeon

Grab any paper. Draw rooms, label enemies with skulls, mark the boss with a crown, connect corridors however you want. It doesn't have to be pretty. That's kind of the point.

Pen + paper only
02

Summon the world

Snap a photo. Gemma 4's vision model reads your sketch and generates a full dungeon — named rooms, lore, enemies with backstories, and a boss with a deep personal grudge.

128K context · Remembers everything
03

Sweat through it

Each monster demands reps. Point your camera, do your squats. AI watches your form — sloppy reps deal less damage, and enemies hit back. Perfect form? Critical hit.

Real reps · Real damage
